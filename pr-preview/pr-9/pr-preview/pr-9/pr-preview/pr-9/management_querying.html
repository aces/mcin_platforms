Behavioural, clinical and neuroimaging data

LORIS is a web-based data and project management software for neuroimaging research studies. It is an open source framework for storing and processing behavioural, clinical, neuroimaging and genetic data.

Clinical data

The Clinical-Biological Imaging and Genetic Repository (the Open Biobank) is a combined patient registry and Open Science collection of biospecimens and clinical information including neuro-psychiatric testing, imaging, and genetic data from patients with neurological diseases as well as healthy controls.

EEG-specific data

EEGNet is an initiative that facilitates national and international collaborative EEG-based neuroscience research. The EEGNet platform is a neuroinformatics hub for data sharing and analytics that combine standardized annotated EEG data and analytics for global health applications.
